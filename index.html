Bat Facts

Bats are a diverse group of mammals ranging from insectivores to vampires. Some pollinate flowers and some dive in lakes and ponds to catch fish They're found all over the world and fall into their own niche category. 1/3 of all mammals are aviators because there are more than 1,300 different species of bat In fact, bats are apart of their own familial group, chiroptera, meaning theyre not part of any other animal family.

Bats use echolocation to find prey and other bats. contrary to popular belief, bats aren't blind, but they do have poor eyesight. Read more!🦇
